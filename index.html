Who We Are

About Orion Innovation

Orion Innovation is a Saudi consulting firm established to accelerate business growth and foster innovation within organizations. Inspired by the Orion constellation, symbolizing strength and future vision, we embody our commitment to enhancing performance, building resilience, driving innovation, achieving harmony, and guiding the way toward delivering integrated consulting solutions that enable organizations to thrive and excel in a rapidly changing business environment.

Our Services

Orion Innovation provides tailored solutions for sustainable growth, empowering organizations with strategies that align with Saudi Vision 2030.

Strategic Consulting

Helping organizations define their vision and long-term goals, develop practical plans to achieve growth, enhance competitiveness, and ensure distinguished sustainability.

Human Resources & Capacity Building

Developing leadership, enhancing individual and organizational capabilities, designing effective human capital policies, and delivering tailored training programs to support a resilient and high-performing workforce.

Innovation & Digital Transformation

Assessing readiness for innovation, identifying improvement areas, and designing practical roadmaps to implement innovative solutions and digital transformation initiatives that boost competitiveness

Management Consulting

Providing expertise in governance and compliance, organizational structuring, policy and procedure design, process reengineering, performance management, and change management to improve operational efficiency.

Sustainability Solutions

Developing strategies to ensure business continuity, maximize positive impact l, and integrate sustainable practices into the core operations of organizations.
